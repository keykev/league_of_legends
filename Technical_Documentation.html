Ionia

Ionia is surrounded by treacherous seas, and the military unit of Ionia is composed of a number of allied provinces that are scattered across an archipelago.

Its culture is shaped by the value that there is balance in all things, thus Ionia has become a place where the border between spiritiual and material is permeable.
